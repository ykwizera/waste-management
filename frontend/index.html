WasteXpert Waste Management System



A platform that helps Hoseholder and Waste collection companies to configure, monitor, and plan everyday waste management activities and be part of a program that fights for a better future through waste management through recycling reusing and re-using.

Householders keep their waste in a way that helps in waste management by keeping their waste separately by their types(meaning where they are recyclable or not) and scheduling the day from our platform for their waste to be collected by waste collection companies.

Waste collection companies will collect the householder's waste depending on the date they scheduled.
